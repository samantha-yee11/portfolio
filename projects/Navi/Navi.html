the problem

NAVI was designed to achieve transparency, clarity, and insight while planning group travel. We have observed that travel planning products are not providing individualized price insights, which is causing frustration in the group planning process. How might we improve NAVI so that our customers are successful based on users committing to a trip they are satisfied with?
